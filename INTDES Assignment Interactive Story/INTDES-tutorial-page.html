Generative AI Tutorial

About Generative AI (Gen AI)

What is Gen AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of teaching machines to mimic human intelligence by learning and adapting through computer programming
Generative AI is a specialized type of AI focused on creating new content such as text, images, and music, rather than just analyzing or processing existing information..

Gen AI VS Search Engines

Search engines like Google or Yahoo locate and list existing web pages based on keywords. Generative AI, however, provides composed answers and allows ongoing interaction, building on previous exchanges to refine responses. This makes it more like chatting with an intelligent assistant capable of generating original and customised outputs.

Popular Gen AI Models

Generative AI is a type of AI that creates original content, like text and images, through interactive, adaptive exchanges, unlike search engines that retrieve existing data.

Capabilities of Generative AI

Gen AI can be used for

1. Text Generation: Write stories, articles, poetry, or create summaries. Example: Use ChatGPT to draft an essay or a social media post.
2. Image Generation: Create unique visuals from scratch. Example: Use tools like DALL-E to design custom artwork or illustrations.
3. Daily Tasks: Getting gooogle Gemini to come up with a diet plan

Using Gen AI effectively

1. Use Natural Language: Write as if chatting with a friend. Example: "Can you summarize this article for me?"
2. Be Specific and Iterative: Clearly explain what you need. Example: "Write a 100-word email about rescheduling a meeting." If the response isn't right, refine your prompt.
3. Be Concise and Clear: Stick to simple, focused instructions. Example: "Generate a list of creative birthday party themes."

Limitations of Gen AI

While Gen AI is able to excute various creative tasks, it cannot do anything physical. Gen AI is also dependent on data quality. Poor-quality data or insufficient training will result in poor outputs

Generative AI excels in creating text, images, and assisting with tasks by using clear, natural, and specific instructions for effective and versatile outputs..

3. Risks and downsides of Gen AI

Inaccuracy and Misinformation

1. Generative AI can sometimes make mistakes or create false information, a phenomenon called "hallucination."
2. It may produce misleading content, like fake news, images, or videos (e.g., deepfakes).
3. Always verify AI-generated content using trusted sources

Privacy Concerns

Using Generative AI can risk your personal or organizational data privacy. Avoid sharing any private, sensitive or confidential information in your prompts.

Copyright Issues

Determining ownership of AI-generated content is challenging as the technology evolves.Generative AI might unintentionally create content that resembles or copies others' work, potentially causing legal issues.

Generative AI poses risks like inaccuracy, privacy concerns, and copyright issues, requiring some degree of caution
